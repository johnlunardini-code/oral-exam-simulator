Help Guide

🏠 Home Page

📚 Pre-load Materials: Upload course syllabi, notes, or slides (PDFs, DOCX, images) to help the tutor understand your course content. Assign to specific courses for targeted feedback.

👤 Your Name: Optional student name for personalized tracking.

🎤 Voice: Choose your preferred text-to-speech voice.

📊 Past Scores: View all your exam attempts and average scores for each course.

🗑️ Clear Scores: Delete all saved exam data (irreversible).

🇮🇹 Italian Exams

Language Support: Italian courses accept responses in Italian or English.

🇮🇹 Risponda in Italiano Button: Appears for Italian courses. Click to hear the professor remind you that you can respond in Italian.

Auto Language Switch: Speech recognition automatically switches to Italian (it-IT) when using the Speak button in Italian exams.

Example: In the Italian course, you can respond using Italian or English, and speech input will be recognized in Italian.

📝 During Exam

Buttons Available:

✓ Send: Submit your typed or spoken answer.

🎤 Speak: Click to start recording, click again to stop. Recognized text appears in the text area.

Mobile Speech: On mobile, you may need to click Speak again if your answer is long. Keep clicking until you are finished speaking, then click Speak once more to submit.

🔊 Mute: Toggle audio on/off. When off, the professor's responses won't be spoken aloud.

📎 Attach: Upload supporting files (PDFs, images, documents) for the tutor to review with your answer.

📊 Get Score: View your current score: number correct, percentage, and Italian 30-point scale.

💡 Hint: Get a study tip and search terms for the current question.

📋 History: View all hints generated during this exam session.

💬 Feedback: Get detailed feedback on your answers by selecting a specific question.

⚙️ Settings: Change voice, test voice output.

End Exam: Finish the exam and save your score.

🎤 Audio Buttons

🎤 Speak: Click to start recording your answer. The text field borders will pulse red. Click again to stop and submit.

Mobile Speech: On mobile, you may need to click Speak again if your answer is long. Keep clicking until you are finished speaking, then click Speak once more to submit.

🔊 Mute: Toggle text-to-speech on/off. Button changes color when muted.

📎 Attach: Upload supporting files with your answer (PDFs, documents, images, audio, video).

⚙️ Settings: Change voice output and test it before your exam.

Note: Voice availability varies by device/browser. Google voices work on most phones. Microsoft voices depend on OS and browser support.

💡 Tips for Success

• Upload course materials on the home page for better AI feedback

• Use hints early to identify weak areas

• Each exam generates fresh questions, so take multiple attempts

• Attach supporting documents to show your reasoning

• Use formatting (bold, italic, lists) to structure complex answers

• For Italian exams, click the Italian button to confirm you can respond in Italian

• Check past scores to track your improvement over multiple attempts

• Works on desktop and mobile devices (iOS, Android)
